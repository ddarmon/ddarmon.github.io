Mid P-values

Mid-\(P\) Confidence Intervals: A Brief Review by Berry and Armitage. \[\begin{aligned} H_{0} &: p \leq 1/2\\ H_{1} &: p > 1/2 \end{aligned}\] \[P\mathrm{-value} = P(X \geq x_{\text{obs}})\] \[\begin{aligned} P\mathrm{-value} &= P(X \geq x_{\text{obs}}) \\ &= 1 - P(X < x_{\text{obs}}) \\ &= 1 - P(X \leq x_{\text{obs}} - 1)\end{aligned}\] n <- 10 p0 <- 0.5 xs <- 0:n Ps <- 1-pbinom(xs-1, n, p0) data.frame(xs, Ps) ## xs Ps ## 1 0 1.
